Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, make a booking, or contact us. This may include:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Payment information
  • Travel preferences and booking history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your bus reservations
  • Send booking confirmations and updates
  • Improve our services
  • Respond to your inquiries

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Bus operators to fulfill your booking
  • Payment processors to handle transactions
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information.

5. Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your account information by logging into your account or contacting us.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@shubha-yatra.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

7. Your Data & Account Deletion Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data. Once deleted, this action cannot be undone.

How to Request Account Deletion:
To permanently delete your Shubha Yatra account and all associated data, please send an email to privacy@shubha-yatra.com from your registered email address with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".

What happens to your data:

  • Your profile, booking history, and personal information will be permanently deleted.
  • Anonymized data may be retained for analytics or legal compliance as permitted by law.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

We aim to process all deletion requests within 14 days of verification.
